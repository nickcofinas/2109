First Chords to Learn

There are a few chords that are simple, easy to learn, and should be your first chords. To learn them click on the basics

The Basics

On top of learning chords, when you first start playing guitar you need to know how to hold the guitar, where to put your hands, and strum

Holding the Guitar

To hold the guitar, you place the instrument on your lap. The ridge of the guitar should be placed comfortably on your knee.

Example

Hand Positions

If you are right handed, the guitar neck will be held with your left hand, and you will strum with your right hand. If left handed, then switch it.

Example

Strumming

Strumming is a vital element of playing guitar. Using a guitar pick is an easy way to start strumming. You want to strum with the same general rythm as your other hand, almost like they are communicating.
